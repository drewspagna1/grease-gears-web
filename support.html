Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add equipment to my truck?

From the dashboard, tap your truck to open it, then tap the + button to add a new piece of equipment. Select the equipment type, give it a name, and save.

How do maintenance reminders work?

When you create or edit a maintenance task, you can set a recurring interval (e.g., every 30 days). The app will send a push notification when that interval is approaching. Make sure notifications are enabled for Grease & Gears in your iPhone's Settings.

How do I log a completed maintenance task?

Tap any task on the dashboard or equipment detail screen, then tap "Log Service." The task resets and your next reminder is scheduled automatically.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same account on any device and your data will sync automatically.

How do I manage my subscription?

Your subscription is managed through the Apple App Store. Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your Grease & Gears subscription.

How do I delete my account and data?

To request account and data deletion, email us at greaseandgears.support@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account." We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

I forgot which email I used to sign up. What should I do?

Try signing in with Google or Facebook if you used one of those providers. If you're still stuck, email us and we can help locate your account.
